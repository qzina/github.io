Our story

Our company was founded by a group of private investors from France. These are people with solid experience both in the seed business and in the development of start-ups in other areas.

Our mission

We research, develop, and market products with intellectual property in plant genetics from extensive crops. We seek to be at the forefront, adding technologies and talents, promoting innovation, associativity, and the development of new businesses that impact our core and the value chain.

Our vision

To be a leading company in plant genetics for extensive crops, present in all the relevant markets worldwide, and sustainably contribute to productivity.
